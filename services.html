Non Alcoholic Beverages

NAHR Al Ahmar General Trading LLC has great experience in supply of branded mineral and sparkling water and a variety of juices and soft drinks.

Due to our close relationships with the suppliers, NAHR is able to deliver directly from the suppliers’ factories on a continual basis, with maximum shelf life, at full container loads, and at prices that are hard to beat.

Food & Confectioneries

NAHR Al Ahmar General Trading LLC specialized in distributing a wide range of food and confectionery products.

We work with all the leading brands to offer the highest quality items in the market. Our wide range of products includes chocolates, cookies, pasta, sauces, candies, snacks, milk powder, and cereals.

Watches and Luxury accessories

Luxury and mid-range watches is one of the fastest growing categories for us.

Kitchenware, Porcelain and Hotel supplies

NAHR is engaged in the distribution of a wide range of kitchenware, homeware, and porcelain.

Apparels
